Tribute to the Music of
Tom Petty & the Heartbreakers

So Petty is a tribute band dedicated to celebrating the music, spirit, and storytelling of Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers. The group formed around 2017, just before Tom Petty's passing in October of that year, and they have been playing his catalog ever since.They perform a wide range of Petty’s music — from classic radio hits to some deeper tracks — capturing the feel and sound that fans know and love.

Upcoming Live Shows

So Petty delivers high-energy performances that faithfully capture the sound and spirit of Tom Petty & the Heartbreakers. From beloved classics like "American Girl" and "Free Fallin’" to deeper fan favorites, the band recreates the iconic hits that defined generations of rock music. Their shows bring audiences together to celebrate the timeless songs and unforgettable live experience that made Tom Petty a legend.
